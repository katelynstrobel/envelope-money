Routing number


Envelope Money accounts use the 123207049 routing number, which is a routing number provided by Pacific West Bank.

The validity of this routing number can be confirmed on The Federal Reserve's website, as well as Pacific West Bank's website.

A "bank verification letter" is also provided in the Documents section of the Envelope Money Account tab.
